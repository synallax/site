Access & integration

Real-time APIs

REST, WebSocket and MCP. Millisecond order handling, engineered by veterans of electronic trading systems.

White-label UI SDK

Order book, bid ticket and "Make an offer" components that drop into your site and apps — with transparent SSO (OIDC/SAML), so your logged-in users are already ours.

Catalog pipelines

Automatic product-catalog import and scheduled sync from your PMS, PSS, DMS or e-commerce stack — with validation and data-quality checks.

Back-office & STP

Fills and settlements stream straight through to your ERP and accounting — reconciliation exports included.

Webhooks & streaming

Every lifecycle event — bid, fill, expiry, quote — pushed in real time to your systems.

Market operations

Pre-trade risk

Spend caps per agent, exposure limits, price collars, velocity limits and a kill switch — nothing enters your book unchecked.

Rules engine

No-code venue configuration: trading hours, TTL bounds, minimum fills, auto-accept thresholds.

Yield autopilot

The seller-side agent: rules or AI that accept or counter bids from your occupancy and utilization signals — connected to your revenue-management system.

Inventory management

Real-time stock sync, overbooking guards, allotments and release schedules.

Lifecycle operations

Busts, corrections, disputes and refund orchestration — with a full paper trail.
